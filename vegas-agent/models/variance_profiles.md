# Variance Profiles

## Why It Matters

Different bet types carry different variance. Same EV does not mean same risk path.

## Profile Buckets

- low variance: high-hit-rate, low-payout structures
- medium variance: balanced hit rate and payout
- high variance: long-tail outcomes, high volatility

## Controls

- high-variance bets require tighter exposure caps
- avoid clustering many high-variance legs on one slate
- evaluate expected drawdown path, not just expected return

## Reporting

Every reviewed card should include:
- variance bucket mix
- concentration warnings
- max expected daily loss under normal conditions
