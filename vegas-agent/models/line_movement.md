# Line Movement Framework

## Movement Types

- opener drift: gradual repricing over time
- sharp shock: sudden, likely information-driven move
- buyback pattern: move then partial reversal
- stale holdout: one book lags market

## Audit Questions

- What changed: information, liquidity, or sentiment?
- Is movement market-wide or isolated?
- Are you late to the move?
- Did your edge improve or evaporate after movement?

## Rules

- Do not chase steam without independent thesis support.
- Heavy adverse movement against your position requires re-audit.
- If movement removes edge buffer, downgrade to `PASS`.
