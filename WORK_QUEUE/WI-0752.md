# WI-0752: NBA Moneyline — verify end-to-end card generation and settlement

**ID**: WI-0752
**Goal**: Confirm the NBA worker actually emits `nba-moneyline-call` cards and that they settle correctly against the `NBA_ML` family in `/results`.

**Background**:
`nba-moneyline-call` exists in `playProducerCardTypes` (route-handler.ts:961), `CARD_FAMILY_MAP` → `NBA_ML`, `MODEL_FAMILY_LABELS` → `NBA Moneyline`, and `MODEL_VERSION_MAP` → `nba-ml-v1`. The full infrastructure is declared. However, it is unknown whether the NBA worker model (`run_nba_model.js` or equivalent) actually generates these cards. No existing cards were observed in `card_payloads` for this card type during the audit.

If the worker doesn't generate these, the family maps are dead declarations and the market is silently missing from both the board and results.

**Scope**:
- `apps/worker/src/jobs/run_nba_model.js` (or equivalent) — investigate whether `nba-moneyline-call` cards are ever emitted; add the generation path if missing
- If generation exists, trace through transform to confirm `MONEYLINE` market type is inferred and the card appears on the board

**Out of scope**:
- Any route-handler or results-page UI changes (infrastructure already in place)

**Acceptance**:
- Running the NBA worker against a game with moneyline odds writes at least one `nba-moneyline-call` row to `card_payloads`
- `GET /api/games` returns that game with a `nba-moneyline-call` play in the MONEYLINE market
- After settlement, `/api/results` includes `NBA_ML` family rows in `segments` with non-zero `settledCards`
