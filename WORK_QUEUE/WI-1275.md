# WI-1275: Market Pulse Opportunity Engine With Projection Overlay And Runtime Tracking

**ID**: WI-1275
**Goal**: Turn Market Pulse from a raw discrepancy feed into a bounded discrepancy-scouting surface that identifies the game, market, side, best observed book price, and model confirmation status where a trustworthy projection exists, while instrumenting the full scan-to-surface lifecycle so we can tell whether the feature is actually useful.

**Why this is worth doing**:
- The current page mostly exposes raw line and price gaps. That is novelty, not a repeatable workflow.
- Users need to answer four questions fast: what game, what market, what side, and why this is worth attention.
- If we do not track what gets scanned, dropped, and surfaced, we cannot tell whether Market Pulse is finding signal or just filtering noise.
- Hourly odds means this surface is for scouting and verification, not live execution.

## Adversarial Constraints

- WI-0811 intentionally shipped a pure detection primitive with no recommendation language. Do not widen the scanner. Recommendation semantics belong in a web-side opportunity layer.
- /api/model-outputs is not universal. NHL is intentionally absent under ADR-0018, and other sports may have incomplete projection coverage. The UI must never fabricate an our projection value when no trustworthy source exists.
- The current route suppresses odds-gap cards for games already flagged with line gaps. WI-1275 must explicitly decide whether to preserve that behavior or replace it with a composite-card model, and tests must lock the chosen behavior.
- Best price is not the same as bet this. Market-only discrepancies must be clearly separated from projection-backed opportunities.
- No DB writes, scheduler changes, or ingestion changes belong in this WI. If the feature only works with persistence or a worker precompute path, the scope is wrong.
- Do not build this as a live betting board. Odds refresh hourly. This is a discrepancy scouting surface. Every opportunity must include freshness status and verify-before-bet language unless odds age is <=15 minutes.

## Odds Freshness Constraint

Odds are fetched approximately once per hour.

Market Pulse must not present opportunities as live actionable bets unless the data is fresh enough and explicitly verified elsewhere.

### Freshness Rules

- `0-15 minutes old` -> `FRESH`
- `16-60 minutes old` -> `STALE_VERIFY_REQUIRED`
- `>60 minutes old` -> `EXPIRED`

### UI Language Rules

Allowed:
- `Best observed price`
- `Market discrepancy`
- `Verify before betting`
- `Projection-aligned watch`
- `Possible stale price`

Forbidden:
- `Bet this`
- `Fire`
- `Hammer`
- `Available now`
- `Lock`
- any copy implying the price is currently live

### Tier Rules Under Hourly Odds

`displayTier` must not imply live execution.

Use:

| Tier | Meaning |
|---|---|
| `SCOUT` | Strong observed discrepancy, verify live before acting |
| `WATCH` | Moderate observed discrepancy |
| `MARKET_ONLY` | Price gap with no projection confirmation |
| `EXPIRED` | Too old to trust |

No `STRONG EDGE` label unless odds freshness is independently verified inside 15 minutes.

### Freshness Overrides Display Tier

displayTier must always be derived AFTER freshnessStatus.

Rules:
- If freshnessStatus = EXPIRED -> displayTier MUST be EXPIRED
- If freshnessStatus = STALE_VERIFY_REQUIRED -> displayTier cannot be SCOUT
- Only FRESH items may be SCOUT

Invalid combinations:
- EXPIRED + SCOUT
- EXPIRED + WATCH
- STALE_VERIFY_REQUIRED + SCOUT

Tests must assert these invalid states cannot occur.

### OpportunityKind vs Display Tier Hierarchy

opportunityKind defines WHAT the data is.
displayTier defines HOW important it is.

Rules:
- MARKET_ONLY items:
  - may be WATCH
  - may NOT be SCOUT unless projectionStatus = CONFIRMED

- PROJECTION_CONFIRMED items:
  - may be SCOUT or WATCH depending on gap + freshness

- CONFLICTING items:
  - must always be WATCH or EXCLUDED (never SCOUT)

- UNSUPPORTED:
  - must not surface as SCOUT or WATCH

### Verify-Before-Bet Label Contract

When freshnessStatus != FRESH:

UI must display:

"Verify before betting — odds may be stale"

Rules:
- exact phrase must be consistent across all cards
- must appear on:
  - STALE_VERIFY_REQUIRED
  - EXPIRED
- must not appear on FRESH items

## Product Rules

- Every surfaced card must identify the game, market, side, best book, comparison reference, recency, and freshness status.
- Projection-backed and market-only cards must be visually and semantically distinct.
- If a projection cannot be joined confidently, the item may still surface as a market discrepancy, but it cannot masquerade as a model-confirmed edge.
- Dedupe must happen at the opportunity layer so the page shows one best card per game, market, line, and side cluster instead of row spam.
- Stale, unsupported, conflicting, and expired items must be labeled explicitly, not silently mixed into the same bucket.
- No UI text may imply live actionable odds unless freshness is independently verified inside 15 minutes.

## Deliverables

### 1. Opportunity Engine

Create a web-side normalization layer that converts raw lineGaps and oddsGaps into a unified MarketPulseOpportunity shape.

Each opportunity should include at minimum:
- gameId, sport, homeTeam, awayTeam
- marketType, displayMarket, line, side
- freshnessStatus: FRESH, STALE_VERIFY_REQUIRED, or EXPIRED
- opportunityKind: PROJECTION_CONFIRMED, MARKET_ONLY, STALE, CONFLICTING, or UNSUPPORTED
- bestBook, bestPrice, referenceBook, referencePrice
- marketGapPct or lineDelta
- capturedAt and minutesAgo
- projectionStatus: CONFIRMED, UNAVAILABLE, MISMATCHED, or UNSUPPORTED_SPORT
- projectionValue or fairPrice only when source data is trustworthy
- displayTier: SCOUT, WATCH, MARKET_ONLY, or EXPIRED
- verifyBeforeBetLabel when freshnessStatus is not FRESH
- suppressionReason when a raw discrepancy is dropped before surfacing

### 2. Projection Overlay

Where a trustworthy model output or canonical projection can be matched to the exact game and market being displayed:
- show the model view next to the market discrepancy
- show whether the model confirms the same side or disagrees
- show the model projection or fair-value reference only when the mapping is explicit and test-covered
- phrase the surfaced item as a projection-aligned watch unless odds freshness is independently verified

If the model join is missing or ambiguous:
- label the card MARKET_ONLY
- do not invent a projection
- do not display imperative copy like bet this

### 3. Composite Opportunity Semantics

Resolve the current line-gap-game-blocks-odds-gap-card behavior one way or the other:
- either preserve it and log that the odds gap was suppressed because the game already has a stronger line-dislocation signal
- or merge both signals into one composite opportunity card

Acceptance requires a deliberate rule with tests. Silent behavior is not acceptable.

### 4. UI Upgrade

Replace generic discrepancy rows with cards that answer the user-facing questions directly.

Each surfaced card should show:
- matchup and market
- best observed price and book
- comparison price and book
- model status: confirmed, unavailable, or disagreement
- freshness status: FRESH, STALE_VERIFY_REQUIRED, or EXPIRED
- verify-before-bet language for any item older than 15 minutes
- tier badge: SCOUT, WATCH, MARKET_ONLY, or EXPIRED
- last updated age

Filters in scope:
- sport
- include or exclude watch-tier cards
- include or exclude stale and expired cards
- show all versus projection-confirmed only

### 5. Runtime Tracking

Add low-overhead route-level observability so each Market Pulse response can be evaluated after launch.

Track at minimum:
- raw line-gap count
- raw odds-gap count
- surfaced opportunity count
- dropped duplicate count
- dropped stale count
- dropped unsupported-projection count
- dropped conflict count
- freshCount
- staleVerifyRequiredCount
- expiredCount
- projectionAlignedWatchCount
- marketOnlyCount
- route duration ms

Emit these as structured logs from the route and include summary metadata in the response for easy manual inspection.

### Observability Enforcement

Route tests MUST assert:

- All required fields exist:
  rawLineGaps, rawOddsGaps, surfaced, droppedDuplicate,
  droppedStale, droppedUnsupported, droppedConflict,
  freshCount, staleVerifyRequiredCount, expiredCount,
  projectionAlignedWatchCount, marketOnlyCount, durationMs

- Missing any field = test failure

- Values must be numbers (not null/undefined)

## Lifecycle Plan

### Build Phase
- Introduce the opportunity-engine transformation layer and types.
- Join projection data only where the mapping is explicit and safe.
- Enforce freshness classification before assigning display tiers or UI copy.
- Upgrade the client to render composite opportunity cards and filters.

### Verification Phase
- Lock the transformation rules with unit tests.
- Lock the route summary counters, freshness counters, and suppression behavior with route tests.
- Manually compare at least one live Market Pulse response against raw discrepancies to confirm the surfaced side and book are accurate.
- Verify that stale and expired rows are labeled correctly and do not read like live opportunities.

### Launch Gate
- Route logs show surfaced versus dropped counts on every request.
- Route logs show `freshCount`, `staleVerifyRequiredCount`, `expiredCount`, `projectionAlignedWatchCount`, and `marketOnlyCount`.
- No card presents projection-backed language for unsupported sports or unmatched rows.
- No UI text implies live actionable odds.
- Duplicate spam is removed.
- Items older than 15 minutes are labeled verify-before-bet.
- Items older than 60 minutes are marked EXPIRED or hidden unless the stale filter allows them.

### Post-Launch Tracking
Initial runtime review should answer:
- Are we surfacing mostly MARKET_ONLY items because projection coverage is weak?
- Are too many rows being dropped as stale or unsupported?
- Are line-gap suppressions hiding odds opportunities we still care about?
- Is route latency still acceptable with the overlay logic?
- How much of the board is FRESH versus STALE_VERIFY_REQUIRED versus EXPIRED?

If those answers are unclear, the next WI should target persisted analytics or settlement feedback. That is not part of WI-1275.

## Acceptance

- Market Pulse no longer presents raw discrepancy rows without context.
- Every surfaced row or card identifies the game, market, side, best book, reference book, age, and freshness status.
- Projection-backed opportunities are explicitly separated from market-only discrepancies.
- Missing or unsupported projections never appear as fake our projection values.
- Dedupe collapses repeated market rows into a single surfaced opportunity per game, market, line, and side cluster.
- Market Pulse copy reflects hourly odds freshness constraints.
- Freshness status deterministically overrides displayTier.
- No invalid freshness/tier combinations can exist.
- opportunityKind and displayTier do not conflict semantically.
- Verify-before-bet label is consistent and enforced.
- Route observability fields are test-validated, not just logged.
- No UI text implies live actionable odds.
- Items older than 15 minutes require verify-before-bet labeling.
- Items older than 60 minutes are marked EXPIRED or hidden unless stale filter is enabled.
- Tests cover fresh, stale-verify-required, and expired odds states.
- The route emits structured lifecycle counters for scanned, dropped, surfaced, and freshness buckets.
- No scanner, ingestion, or DB-writer scope is added.

## Manual Validation

- Open /market-pulse and confirm each surfaced item shows game, market, side, book, recency, and freshness status.
- Compare at least one MARKET_ONLY card and one projection-backed card against raw route data and confirm the UI classification is correct.
- Verify that unsupported sports or unmatched rows do not display fabricated model projections.
- Verify stale-filter behavior by forcing or mocking an aged snapshot.
- Verify that anything older than 15 minutes reads as verify before betting.
- Verify that anything older than 60 minutes is marked EXPIRED or hidden unless the stale filter is enabled.
- Inspect route logs for surfaced, dropped, and freshness counters after loading the page.

## Failure Modes

- Projection join logic maps the wrong model row to the displayed market.
- Composite suppression hides legitimate price opportunities without an explicit reason.
- Route instrumentation is too vague to explain why surfaced counts are low.
- UI language overstates confidence for market-only discrepancies.
- UI accidentally implies live odds despite hourly refresh cadence.
- Scope creep pulls scanner or worker changes into a web-only surface rewrite.
