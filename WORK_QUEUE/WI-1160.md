# WI-1160: Canonical SLIGHT EDGE Path Mapping And Suppression Inventory

**ID**: WI-1160
**Goal**: Produce a canonical end-to-end map of how a candidate becomes `SLIGHT EDGE`, where real lean signals are hidden or mislabeled, and which suppression branches most likely keep thin-but-real edges from surfacing cleanly.

**Requirements**: [EDGE-PATH-01, EDGE-PATH-02, EDGE-PATH-03]

**Roadmap traceability**:

- Planning prerequisite for `discord-hook-eligibility` implementation work.
- Must complete before WI-1162 claim/implementation begins.

**Scope**:

- `.planning/debug/slight-edge-path-map.md`
- `WORK_QUEUE/WI-1160.md`

**Out of scope**:

- Reclassifying lean thresholds or changing decision math
- Renaming public labels or redesigning card presentation
- Refactoring unrelated transform or posting paths

**Acceptance**:

- [EDGE-PATH-01] The mapping document traces the canonical `LEAN -> SLIGHT EDGE` path across worker payloads, web transform/decision-surface logic, UI rendering, filters, and Discord/webhook bucketing.
- [EDGE-PATH-02] The document inventories all known status/token aliases involved in this path, including `LEAN`, `WATCH`, `HOLD`, `SLIGHT EDGE`, and `lean`, and explains where each alias is authoritative.
- [EDGE-PATH-03] The document lists suppression or demotion branches that can hide a real lean, including at minimum verification downgrades, missing-odds demotion, projection-only exclusion, non-actionable non-total suppression, and Discord lean suppression/minimum-edge handling where applicable.
- The document calls out reason-label and surfaced-reason fallback behavior that can make a lean appear as `No edge at current price` or other PASS-like copy even when the underlying status is not PASS.
- The document ends with a ranked set of follow-up implementation slices based on likelihood of causing current SLIGHT EDGE overuse or PLAY starvation.
