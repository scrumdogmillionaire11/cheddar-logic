# WI-0940: NHL Blocking Remediation for Market Calls

**ID**: WI-0940
**Goal**: Identify and fix NHL market-call blocking paths that suppress actionable cards beyond intended policy, while preserving intentional risk controls and making all blocks explicitly diagnosable.

**Depends on**: WI-0939

**Scope**:

- `apps/worker/src/jobs/run_nhl_model.js`
- `packages/models/src/decision-pipeline-v2.js`
- `apps/worker/src/utils/decision-publisher.js`
- `apps/worker/src/jobs/__tests__/run_nhl_model.market-calls.test.js`
- `apps/worker/src/jobs/__tests__/execution-gate-decision-consistency.test.js`
- `web/src/lib/games/route-handler.ts`
- `docs/audits/nhl-blocking-audit.md`

**Out of scope**:

- Broad NHL projection-model formula redesign
- New sport onboarding or non-NHL market additions
- Discord copy/layout changes unrelated to block semantics

**Acceptance**:

- For NHL totals and moneyline calls, every blocked candidate is classified into an explicit reason family (pricing freshness, edge insufficiency, support insufficiency, integrity/certainty veto, market contract mismatch).
- `decision_v2.official_status`, action/classification, and block reason fields are internally consistent for blocked rows.
- No silent drop path remains between model decision and persisted card payload for NHL market calls.
- Diagnostic counters are available per reason family for the latest run and are queryable from logs or audit output.
- Regression tests cover at least one previously over-blocked path and one intentionally blocked path to prove policy boundaries.
