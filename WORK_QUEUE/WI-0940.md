# WI-0940: NHL Blocking Remediation for Market Calls

**ID**: WI-0940
**Goal**: Identify and fix NHL market-call blocking paths that suppress actionable cards beyond intended policy, while preserving intentional risk controls, removing blocking-related tech debt or legacy branches encountered in-scope, and making all blocks explicitly diagnosable.

**Depends on**: WI-0939

**Architecture constraints (must hold)**:

- ADR-0004 principles govern decision ownership for this WI: worker remains decision authority; downstream layers consume canonical decision payloads and must not recompute verdicts for NHL market calls.
- DATA_CONTRACTS invariants apply: one candidate, one deterministic terminal reason family; no silent drops between decision, persisted card payload, and surfaced game-card representation.
- Any retained compatibility fallback in web or publish layers must be explicitly documented in the audit as intentional debt; no new implicit fallback paths may be introduced.

**Enumerated tech debt to address in this WI**:

- TD-01: Multiple post-publish terminal-status mutation points in `run_nhl_model.js` (`applyUiActionFields`, uncertainty hold, execution-gate demotion, totals-policy remap, no-odds override) can overwrite each other and leave contradictory state surfaces across all three NHL market-call card types.
- TD-02: Branch-specific last-write-wins overrides in the market-call card loop (inline `classification`/`action`/`official_status` reassignments applied *after* `applyUiActionFields`) and stale selector-era comments obscure which NHL market was blocked intentionally versus mutated after publish. Note: `choosePrimaryDisplayMarket` at line 2724 and the `buildDualRunRecord` / `[DUAL_RUN]` infrastructure are observability-only and must **not** be removed; TD-02 targets only the post-`applyUiActionFields` inline mutation overrides and dead/misleading comments.
- TD-03: Worker/web contract drift where blocked NHL market-call semantics can be reconstructed from fallback fields in `route-handler.ts` instead of consuming one canonical persisted reason surface.
- TD-04: Diagnostics gap where blocked NHL totals, spread, and moneyline candidates are not summarized into deterministic reason families with per-market counters comparable to MLB WI-0939 output.
- TD-05: Stale documentation/comments describing deprecated or compatibility-only blocking behavior after independent market evaluation migration.

**Acceptance**:

- For all three NHL market-call card types (`nhl-totals-call`, `nhl-spread-call`, `nhl-moneyline-call`), every blocked candidate is classified into an explicit reason family (pricing freshness, edge insufficiency, support insufficiency, integrity/certainty veto, market contract mismatch).
- `decision_v2.official_status`, action/classification, and block reason fields are internally consistent for blocked rows across all three card types.
- No silent drop path remains between model decision and persisted card payload for NHL market calls.
- Diagnostic counters are available per reason family and per card type for the latest run and are queryable from logs or audit output.
- Regression tests cover at least one previously over-blocked path and one intentionally blocked path to prove policy boundaries.
- Tech-debt closeout is objective and complete:
  - TD-01: parity assertions prove NHL market-call payloads cannot persist contradictory terminal status/action/classification fields after any demotion or policy remap.
  - TD-02: explicit list of removed post-publish last-write-wins override blocks and stale selector-era comments is recorded in the audit doc with `rg` absence proof; `choosePrimaryDisplayMarket` observability call and `buildDualRunRecord` / `[DUAL_RUN]` logging infrastructure are explicitly retained as intentional and listed in the debt ledger as `retained-intentional`.
  - TD-03: web transform assertions prove blocked NHL market calls surface the canonical persisted reason/status instead of inventing fallback verdicts when canonical fields exist.
  - TD-04: deterministic health or audit diagnostics include per-market reason-family counters for all three NHL market-call card types and no unexplained uncategorized blocker bucket without a named source row.
  - TD-05: outdated comments/runbook statements are removed or rewritten; retained compatibility debt is listed in the ledger with rationale and follow-up owner.
