**Acceptance**:

- Projection accuracy capture stores immutable `projection_raw` and synthetic nearest-half grading fields using insert-ignore semantics.
- Settlement grades deterministic synthetic W/L/PUSH, flags missing actuals and projection/synthetic mutations, computes MAE and signed bias, and excludes `DIRECTION_TOO_WEAK` rows from directional W/L.
- Confidence score, confidence band, Poisson expected probability, calibration bucket, common-line simulations, and market trust status are available for the four requested market families.
- `/api/results/projection-accuracy` returns read-only accuracy, calibration, confidence, weak-direction, and market-trust data.
- `/results` clearly renders projection confidence and market health without implying odds-backed P&L.
