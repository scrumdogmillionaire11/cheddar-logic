## Context

`buildClvEntryFromPendingCard()` at [apps/worker/src/jobs/settle_pending_cards.js](apps/worker/src/jobs/settle_pending_cards.js#L81) uses `lockedMarket.lockedPrice` for `odds_at_pick`. Since `prepareModelAndCardWrite` deletes and reinserts on every 30-min cycle, by settlement time `lockedPrice` reflects the last-run odds snapshot â€” not the original pick price. Opening line advantage (the clearest indicator of skill) is therefore unmeasurable.
