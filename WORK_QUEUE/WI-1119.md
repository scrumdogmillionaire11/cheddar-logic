# WI-1119: Harden F5 Totals Range-Scored Proxy Policy

**ID**: WI-1119
**Goal**: Prevent ambiguous F5 totals from being evaluated against a single guessed line by separating binary proxy, weak proxy, and range-only telemetry.

**Scope**:
- Covered by active umbrella execution scope in `WORK_QUEUE/WI-1117.md`

**Out of scope**:

**Acceptance**:
- F5 totals use common lines `3.5`, `4.0`, and `4.5`.
- Strong binary proxy applies only at `<=3.1` and `>=4.9`.
- Weak proxy applies at `3.1-3.4` and `4.6-4.9`.
- Ambiguous range-only rows are tracked but excluded from binary proxy hit rate.
