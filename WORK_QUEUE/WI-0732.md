**Acceptance**:
- F5 totals are based on weighted `SIERA/xFIP/xERA`, opponent handedness splits, xwOBA/contact overlays, park/weather, projected starter F5 IP, and a TTOP penalty.
- ERA-only inputs do not produce `FULL_MODEL`; they fall to `SYNTHETIC_FALLBACK` with a PASS cap and a neutral floor instead of ERA math.
- `mlb-f5` payloads include `projection_source`, `status_cap`, extended `projection` fields, `playability`, and `missing_inputs`.
- Missing non-core inputs such as weather or TTOP produce `DEGRADED_MODEL` with a LEAN/WATCH cap; missing core starter/matchup/park inputs produce `SYNTHETIC_FALLBACK` with PASS semantics.
- `abs(edge) < 0.5` produces a visible `PASS` F5 card with reason/playability context instead of a forced play.
- Web transform preserves the new F5 projection provenance and playable-range fields for render consumers.
- `docs/decisions/ADR-0007-mlb-f5-full-model-projection-contract.md` documents the model/data contract and fallback policy.
