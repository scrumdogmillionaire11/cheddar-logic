# WI-0483: Fix nba-blowout-risk role and document unclassified NHL cardTypes in DRIVER_ROLES

**ID**: WI-0483
**Goal**: Correct `nba-blowout-risk` from its silent CONTEXT default to `RISK` in `DRIVER_ROLES`, and explicitly register unclassified NHL cardTypes so their scoring intent is documented.

**Depends on**: none

**Scope**:
- `web/src/lib/game-card/driver-scoring.ts`
- `WORK_QUEUE/WI-0483.md`

**Out of scope**:
- Changing gate thresholds (`GATE` constants)
- Adding new cardTypes to the worker models
- Registry completeness enforcement (see WI-0484)

**Background**:
During WI-0479 test investigation, `nba-blowout-risk` was found to be absent from `DRIVER_ROLES`. Because unknown types default to `CONTEXT`, this card type silently participates in `net_support` scoring (reducing it when contra-aligned, boosting when aligned). A blowout risk signal should never affect play score — it should only surface as a risk code. Additionally, several NHL cardTypes emitted by the worker are unregistered and rely on the CONTEXT fallback. While CONTEXT is correct for most of them, the intent is undocumented and invisible to future contributors.

**Acceptance**:
1. `nba-blowout-risk` is registered as `RISK` in `DRIVER_ROLES`.
2. The following NHL types are explicitly registered as `CONTEXT`:
   - `nhl-base-projection`
   - `nhl-goalie`
   - `nhl-goalie-certainty`
   - `nhl-lineup`
   - `nhl-shot-environment`
   - `nhl-model-output`
3. `nhl-player-shots` and `nhl-player-shots-1p` are registered — role determined by inspecting their payload direction field (OVER/UNDER → may warrant PRIMARY for totals plays; otherwise CONTEXT).
4. No scoring behavior changes for cards that had no `nba-blowout-risk` driver (non-breaking).
5. Cards with a `nba-blowout-risk` driver aligned to play direction no longer inflate `net_support`.
