# WI-0588: Quarantine NBA totals by demoting actionable tiers one level

**ID**: WI-0588
**Goal**: Apply an immediate conservative production quarantine to NBA totals so current actionable statuses are demoted one tier while diagnostic and calibration work is underway.

**Scope**:
- `packages/models/src/decision-pipeline-v2.js`
- `packages/models/src/decision-pipeline-v2.patch.js` (if quarantine is implemented as a sport/market override or feature-flagged profile)
- `apps/worker/src/jobs/run_nba_model.js`
- `packages/models/src/__tests__/edge-calculator.test.js` (if reason-code/status assertions land there)
- `web/src/__tests__/api-results-decision-segmentation.test.js`

**Out of scope**:
- Rewriting the NBA totals projection formula
- Retuning NBA spread or moneyline markets
- Permanent NBA totals threshold numbers (track after diagnostics)

**Background**:
Production NBA totals are `14-21` and `-8.35u` across PLAY + SLIGHT EDGE. That is too weak to keep treating the market as fully trusted. Immediate operational feedback is to demote the market now rather than let the current tiering continue to allocate full PLAY exposure while the root cause is still unknown.

**Acceptance**:
- NBA `TOTAL` decisions cannot surface as `PLAY` while the quarantine is enabled.
- NBA `TOTAL` decisions that would previously resolve to `PLAY` are downgraded to `LEAN`.
- NBA `TOTAL` decisions that would previously resolve to `LEAN` are downgraded to `PASS`.
- The demotion emits a concrete reason code or flag so the quarantine is visible in logs/payloads.
- The quarantine applies only to `sport = NBA` and `market_type = TOTAL`; NHL totals and NBA spreads remain unchanged.
- The implementation is reversible via a clearly bounded switch or isolated rule block once follow-on diagnostics are complete.
