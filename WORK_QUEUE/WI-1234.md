## Summary
Refine `WI-1234` into a narrow follow-up for the `/wedge` client-state bug where the Active lifecycle view stops surfacing cards after games begin. Keep this WI strictly in the cards client layer and make it depend on `WI-1232` for any backend/API visibility defects.

## Key Changes
- Replace the stub with a full work item whose goal is: preserve `/wedge` Active-mode visibility across hydration, lifecycle toggles, retries, and filter interactions without changing decision authority or `/api/games` contracts.
- Scope only:
  - `web/src/components/cards/CardsPageContext.tsx`

  - `web/src/lib/game-card/filters.ts`
