## Background

`risk_analysis.ipynb` concluded the strategy is **ROBUST** at a +3pp assumed edge with quarter-Kelly staking. That conclusion is only valid if the model actually produces +3pp net edge after vig.

The risk translation in the original analysis stated explicitly:

> *"Robustness depends on true edge accuracy. Current model likely inflates edge via miscalibration and redundancy. Risk model is optimistic; real drawdowns and ruin probability understated."*

WI-0825 and WI-0831 will, for the first time, produce a stream of calibrated `fair_prob` values alongside actual outcomes. This WI closes the loop: take those measurements, compute an empirical edge distribution, and re-run the risk model.
