# WI-0646: Playoff-mode detection and model adjustment for NBA and NHL

**ID**: WI-0646
**Goal**: Add a `isPlayoffGame(game)` flag (derived from ESPN `season.type` or game metadata) and use it to apply playoff-specific threshold overrides — tighter sigma, higher edge minimums, reduced pace factor weight — in `run_nba_model.js` and `run_nhl_model.js`.

**Background**:
NBA playoffs begin ~April 19, 2026; NHL playoffs begin ~April 22, 2026. Neither model has any playoff-context awareness. Regular-season pace, empirical sigma, and spread/total sigma calibrations are miscalibrated for playoff conditions (slower pace, tighter lines, higher variance per team). Cards generated in rounds 1–4 without this context will have systematically mis-sized edges.

**Scope**:
- `apps/worker/src/jobs/run_nba_model.js`
- `apps/worker/src/jobs/run_nhl_model.js`
- `packages/data/src/season-gates.js` or equivalent (add `isPlayoffGame()` helper)
- `apps/worker/src/__tests__/run-nba-model.test.js` (extend once WI-0644 is done, or include here if WI-0644 is not yet started)
- `apps/worker/src/__tests__/run-nhl-model.test.js` (extend or create)

**Out of scope**:
- UI changes for "playoff mode" labeling
- MLB playoff handling (MLB playoffs are October — out of season)
- NFL (handled by seasonal gate already)
- Prop models (SOG/pitcher K) — separate calibration effort

**Acceptance**:
- `isPlayoffGame(game)` returns `true` when `season.type === 3` (ESPN playoff code) or `game.gameType === 'P'`
- NBA model: when `isPlayoff === true`, applies `PLAYOFF_SIGMA_MULTIPLIER` (default: 1.2), `PLAYOFF_EDGE_MIN` (default: +0.01 above regular), pace factor capped at 0.5 weight
- NHL model: same contract with NHL-appropriate defaults
- Both models log `[PLAYOFF_MODE]` flag when active
- `npm --prefix apps/worker test -- --testPathPattern=run-nba-model|run-nhl-model` passes including playoff fixture scenarios
- `npm --prefix apps/worker test` exits 0
