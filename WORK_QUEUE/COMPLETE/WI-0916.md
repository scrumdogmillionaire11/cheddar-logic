# WI-0916: Sport-Specific News Impact Translation + Input Gate Integration

**ID**: WI-0916
**Goal**: Translate normalized news signals into deterministic per-sport model context, threshold caps, and recompute requirements without allowing news to fabricate recommendations or bypass existing edge logic.

**Depends on**: WI-0915

**Scope**:

- `apps/worker/src/jobs/run_nfl_model.js`

**Out of scope**:

- Scheduler polling mechanics and target resolution
- Final watchdog invalidation and payload-schema enforcement
- Introducing any new public recommendation status vocabulary
- New standalone playoff or news-only recommendation engines

**Acceptance**:

- `compute-news-impact.js` provides deterministic per-sport translation rules for NHL/NBA/NFL/MLB using normalized news inputs only.
- `input-gate.js` exposes a pure `evaluateNewsGate(game)`-style contract that returns `allow_model_run`, `max_status`, `reason_codes`, and `recompute_required` semantics.
- NHL translation accounts for goalie confirmation/swap, key skater status, and special-teams-style sensitivity without reintroducing raw-source logic into the pace model.
- NBA translation accounts for lineup confirmation, late scratches, rotation shrink, and minutes-limit effects.
- NFL translation accounts for weather, inactives/cluster changes, and travel/rest-style structural news.
- MLB translation accounts for starter confirmation/scratch, bullpen state, lineup concentration, and weather/roof context.
- News can enrich context, tighten thresholds, or force recomputation, but it cannot directly generate `PLAY`/`LEAN`/`PASS` decisions on its own.
