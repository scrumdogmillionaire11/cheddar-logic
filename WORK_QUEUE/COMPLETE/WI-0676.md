# WI-0676: Allow legal cross-position sanity swaps in starting XI optimizer

**ID**: WI-0676
**Goal**: Fix the starting XI “projection sanity swap” so a higher-projected bench outfielder can replace a lower-projected starter from another outfield position when the resulting shape is still a legal FPL formation.

**Background**:
After fixing `WI-0675`, the full `cheddar-fpl-sage` suite exposed `tests/tests_new/test_formation_feasibility.py::test_projection_sanity_swap_promotes_higher_projected_midfielder`.
