# WI-0941: NBA Blocking Remediation and Quarantine Boundary Cleanup

**ID**: WI-0941
**Goal**: Separate intentional NBA blocking policy from accidental over-blocking, then fix non-policy block paths so actionable NBA markets can surface deterministically with transparent reason codes.

**Depends on**: WI-0939

**Scope**:

- `packages/models/src/decision-pipeline-v2-edge-config.js`
- `packages/models/src/flags.js`
- `apps/worker/src/jobs/run_nba_model.js`
- `apps/worker/src/jobs/__tests__/report_telemetry_calibration.test.js`
- `packages/models/src/__tests__/decision-pipeline-v2-nba-total-quarantine.test.js`
- `web/src/lib/game-card/filters.ts`
- `docs/audits/nba-blocking-audit.md`

**Out of scope**:

- Permanent retirement of NBA totals quarantine policy
- New NBA market families
- UI redesign of cards page presets

**Acceptance**:

- NBA block reasons are partitioned into policy blocks (for example quarantine) vs operational blocks (stale/missing price, edge/support fail, execution gate).
- For each NBA market family in scope, produced vs surfaced counts are explainable by explicit reason codes only.
- Any duplicated or contradictory demotion path (status/action/classification disagreement) is removed.
- Quarantine behavior remains explicit and reversible via config with test coverage proving on/off behavior.
- Audit output includes per-market reason distribution and identifies top avoidable blockers.
