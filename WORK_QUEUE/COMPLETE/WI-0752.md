# WI-0752: NBA Spread — verify end-to-end board display and settlement

**ID**: WI-0752
**Goal**: Confirm `nba-spread-call` cards surface correctly on the board and settle against the `NBA_SPREAD` family in `/results`.

**Background**:
`nba-spread-call` is fully wired in the worker (`run_nba_model.js` lines 652, 809, 1120), in `playProducerCardTypes` (route-handler.ts:963), `CARD_FAMILY_MAP` → `NBA_SPREAD`, `MODEL_FAMILY_LABELS` → `NBA Spread`, `MODEL_VERSION_MAP` → `nba-spread-v1`, and `driver-scoring.ts` (PRIMARY role). As of 2026-04-03 there are **249 rows** in `card_payloads` for this card type, so generation is confirmed working.

The open question is end-to-end visibility: do these cards correctly appear on `/cards` under the SPREAD market, and does `/api/results` return non-zero `settledCards` for `NBA_SPREAD` once games settle?

**Scope**:
- `apps/worker/src/jobs/run_nba_model.js` — confirm spread edge threshold, action logic, and card shape are correct (no silent no-ops at the edge gate)
- `web/src/lib/games/route-handler.ts` — confirm `nba-spread-call` is in `playProducerCardTypes` and `CARD_FAMILY_MAP` / `MARKET_TYPE_MAP` map it to `SPREAD`
- `web/src/app/api/results/projection-metrics.ts` — confirm `NBA_SPREAD` family is in `CARD_FAMILY_MAP`, `MODEL_FAMILY_LABELS`, and `MODEL_VERSION_MAP`
- `apps/worker/src/jobs/__tests__/run_nba_model.market-calls.test.js` — existing spread tests; extend coverage if gaps found

**Out of scope**:
- Any UI style changes to the cards board
