**Acceptance**:
- Pitcher K projections are derived from expected batters faced and an opponent-adjusted K interaction rate, not raw K/9 or last-5 Ks alone:
  - `bf_exp = projected_ip * batters_per_inning`, where batters per inning adjusts for starter BB%, starter xwOBA allowed, and opponent OBP/contact quality
  - `k_interaction = starter_K% * opp_K%_vs_hand / lg_K%` as v1, with SwStr%, F-Strike%, zone%, and pitch-mix whiff overlays staged for v1.1
  - `k_mean = bf_exp * k_interaction * k_leash_mult`
- Mandatory Pitcher K inputs are logged in this WI and must be modeled explicitly:
  - projected batters faced / projected IP / leash
  - starter K%, SwStr% or a whiff proxy, handedness
  - opponent K% vs hand and contact profile
- Banned primary drivers are logged in this WI:
  - K/9 alone
  - average IP alone
  - last-5 strikeouts alone
- Pitcher K payloads include a Poisson v1 probability ladder for at least `P(5+)`, `P(6+)`, and `P(7+)`, over/under fair prices, playable thresholds, projection source, status cap, and missing-input metadata.
- Synthetic or incomplete-input pitcher K projections cannot emit PLAY/LEAN semantics and remain visible only as PASS / projection-only outputs.
- No-edge pitcher K outcomes are not promoted into actionable cards when probability edge is below the configured threshold/noise band.
- ADR-0008 documents the pitcher K model contract, fallback policy, and rollout constraints.
