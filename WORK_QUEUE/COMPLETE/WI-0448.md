# WI-0448: Settlement + Segmentation Record Traceability Audit

**ID**: WI-0448
**Goal**: Audit end-to-end settlement and segmentation flows and enforce record-level traceability so every play record can be tied to play type, call, projection source, and driver lineage.

**Scope**:

- `WORK_QUEUE/WI-0448.md`
- `docs/DATA_CONTRACTS.md`
- `docs/ops-runbook.md`
- `web/src/app/api/cards/route.ts`
- `web/src/app/api/games/route.ts`
- `web/src/app/api/results/route.ts`
- `apps/worker/src/jobs/**` (only files directly involved in settlement + segmentation lineage)
- `packages/data/src/**` (only read/write paths that persist or expose settlement/segmentation lineage fields)
- `scripts/` (optional lightweight audit script(s) for reproducible record-level verification)

**Out of scope**:

- Rebuilding model math or projection algorithms
- UI redesign unrelated to audit visibility of settlement/segmentation lineage
- Non-essential refactors or repo-wide formatting
- New betting products/markets

**Acceptance**:

- A written lineage map exists for canonical record lifecycle: ingest -> projection -> card generation -> settlement/result.
- For each canonical play record, audit confirms traceable fields for: `sport`, `market_type`/play type, call/action, projection source/run, and driver context.
- Segmentation buckets used by reads/reports are enumerated and mapped to source-of-truth fields (no ambiguous/implicit segmentation).
- Any gaps are classified as: `contract gap`, `write-path gap`, `read-path gap`, or `naming drift`, with concrete fix recommendations.
- At least one reproducible verification command/report demonstrates per-record traceability coverage and flags missing lineage.
- Docs/runbook are updated with the audit procedure and triage steps for missing lineage fields.
