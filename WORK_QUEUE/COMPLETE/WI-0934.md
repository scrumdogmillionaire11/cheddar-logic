# WI-0934: NHL totals bucket policy enforcement — PLAY / SLIGHT EDGE / PASS

**ID**: WI-0934
**Goal**: Enforce the correct PLAY / SLIGHT EDGE / PASS bucket mapping for NHL totals so that high-delta edges (e.g. +2.1 goals) surface as PLAY, not "Slight Edge." This is a wrapper/output-policy fix, not a projection-model change.

**Depends on**: WI-0931, WI-0932, WI-0933

**Scope**:

- `apps/worker/src/jobs/post_discord_cards.js`
- `apps/worker/src/jobs/__tests__/post_discord_cards.test.js`

**Out of scope**:

- NHL pace model math or projection logic
- Decision pipeline v2 internals
- execution-gate.js (integrity gate already complete from WI-0933)
- MLB/NBA market bucketing

**Acceptance**:

- NHL total card with edge >= 1.0, no integrity failure, no uncertainty hold, no fragility penalty → surfaces in 🟢 PLAY section (official bucket).
- NHL total card with 0.5 <= edge < 1.0 → surfaces in lean section (🟡 Slight Edge or higher header).
- NHL total card with edge < 0.35 → pass_blocked.
- Mixed-book integrity block (MIXED_BOOK_INTEGRITY_GATE reason code) → pass_blocked regardless of edge.
- Goalie/injury uncertainty hold → caps status at SLIGHT EDGE even for large edges.
- OVER on line >= 6.5 without accelerant_score >= 0.20 → caps PLAY to SLIGHT EDGE.
- UNDER on line <= 5.5 → unconditional one-tier downgrade (PLAY→SLIGHT EDGE, SLIGHT EDGE→PASS).
- Conviction tier labels (Play-Grade Edge, Strong Play Edge, etc.) remain in render body — subordinate to final_status.
- Regression: +2.1 over 5.5 clean → PLAY; +0.5 over 5.5 → SLIGHT EDGE; never equal-presented.
