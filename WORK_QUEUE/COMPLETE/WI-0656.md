**Acceptance**:
- `POST /api/v1/draft-sessions/{id}/audit` returns structure, philosophy fit, captaincy strength, template exposure, fragility, correlation exposure, exit liquidity, time-to-fix, and “what breaks this team”.
- `POST /api/v1/draft-sessions/compare` accepts two normalized squads or two draft-session references and returns a winner plus delta summary.
- Audit scoring is profile-aware; the same squad can receive materially different commentary under different archetypes.
- Comparison output explains tradeoffs instead of only returning a numeric winner.
