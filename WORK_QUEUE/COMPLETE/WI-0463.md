# WI-0463: Skater injury factor in NHL totals model

**ID**: WI-0463
**Goal**: Make `predictNHLGame()` injury-aware so confirmed-out skaters reduce expected goals before pace adjustments.

**Scope**:

- `WORK_QUEUE/WI-0463.md`
- `apps/worker/src/models/index.js`
- `apps/worker/src/models/nhl-pace-model.js`
- `apps/worker/src/models/__tests__/nhl-pace-model.test.js`

**Out of scope**:

- Weighted player quality adjustments (tracked in WI-0464)

- Defense-side injury modeling

**Acceptance**:

- `computeSkaterInjuryFactor(injuries)` returns `null` when no confirmed-out skaters are present.
- One confirmed-out skater yields factor `0.965`; three yields `0.895`; capped minimum is `0.88`.
- DTD/Questionable statuses are ignored by confirmed-out logic.
- `predictNHLGame()` accepts `homeSkaterInjuryFactor` / `awaySkaterInjuryFactor` and applies them before pace adjustments.
- `adjustments.home.skater_injury` / `adjustments.away.skater_injury` are populated when factors are applied.
- `apps/worker/src/models/__tests__/nhl-pace-model.test.js` passes with WI-0463 coverage.
