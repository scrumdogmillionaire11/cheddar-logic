# WI-1241: Schedule Projection Health Materialization

**ID**: WI-1241
**Goal**: Keep projection-health summaries fresh by wiring the existing health/materialization job into the scheduler and exposing staleness through health reads.

**Context**:

`projection_accuracy_health` exists but is not scheduler-driven, so production market-health reads can go stale even while settlement is current. This WI makes projection-health freshness an operational contract.

**Scope**:
- `apps/worker/src/jobs/projection_accuracy_health.js`
- `apps/worker/src/schedulers/main.js`
- `web/src/app/api/admin/model-health/route.ts`
- `web/src/app/api/admin/pipeline-health/route.ts`
- `apps/worker/src/__tests__/projection-accuracy-health.scheduler.test.js` (new)
- `web/src/__tests__/api-admin-projection-health-freshness.test.js` (new)
- `WORK_QUEUE/WI-1241.md`

**Out of scope**:
- Any change to calibration thresholds
- Any expansion of projection family scope
- Any decision-policy tuning

**Acceptance**:
- Projection health/materialization runs on a defined schedule after the relevant settlement stages.
- Health reads include freshness timestamps and an explicit stale indicator.
- Staleness breaches are detectable through an API-visible signal.
- Regression coverage proves the scheduled path runs and stale thresholds are surfaced.
