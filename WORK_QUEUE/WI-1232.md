**Out of scope**:
- Any model threshold, scoring, or decision production changes that alter when rows become `Play`, `Slight Edge`, or `Pass`
- Any backfill of `card_display_log` at settlement time unless there is proof the card was shown live
- Allowing modern rows without canonical `decision_v2` onto `/wedge`
- Mixing projection-only rows into main `/results`
- Worker fail-closed semantics or settlement health status policy changes (owned by follow-up health WIs)

**Acceptance**:
- A 7-day audit can deterministically classify recent rows into exactly one missing-row reason: `MISSING_DECISION_V2`, `NOT_DISPLAY_ELIGIBLE`, `DISPLAY_LOG_NOT_ENROLLED`, `API_FILTERED`, `UI_LABEL_MISMATCH`, `PROJECTION_ONLY`, `VALID_PASS`, or `BUG`
- Audit output is captured in `docs/audits/card-visibility-integrity-2026-05-04.md`, including SQL used, count table, and representative card IDs for each bucket
- Valid modern `Play` and `Slight Edge` rows with canonical `decision_v2` enroll into `card_display_log` when display-eligible
- Enrolled modern `Play` and `Slight Edge` rows are returned by `/api/games` unless filtered by a proven, intended contract
- Main `/results` continues to exclude settled odds-backed rows that lack `card_display_log` proof
- Projection-only `mlb-f5` / `mlb-f5-ml` rows stay out of main `/results` and remain visible on `/results/projections`
- UI copy makes `Slight Edge` semantics clear where the current `HOLD` / `WATCH` wording is ambiguous
