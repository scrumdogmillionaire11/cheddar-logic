# WI-1257: Add Projection-Only Portfolio Guardrails for MLB Pitcher-K

**ID**: WI-1257
**Goal**: Reduce variance stacking in projection-only recommendations with slip-level clustering and leash-correlation guardrails.

**Context**:

Even without executable betting calls, candidate-zone outputs can still encourage over-clustered exposures unless risk controls are explicit.

**Scope**:
- `apps/worker/src/jobs/run_mlb_model.js`
- `apps/worker/src/jobs/__tests__/run_mlb_model.test.js`
- `docs/pitcher_ks/07output.md`
- `WORK_QUEUE/WI-1257.md`

**Out of scope**:
- Live pricing engines
- External odds APIs
- Auto-publication of actionable betting recommendations

**Acceptance**:
- Configurable controls cap same-slate pitcher-K clustering and leg count.
- Hard clustering rules enforced:
  - max 2 pitcher-K candidates per slate may carry actionable posture labels
  - max 1 pitcher-K candidate per game
  - if >2 UNDER_CANDIDATE exist on the same slate: downgrade all to WATCH
- Correlation warnings are emitted for fragile constructions (for example short-leash clusters).
- Reason codes/audit fields show why recommendations were capped, downgraded, or withheld.
- Tests verify guardrail behavior for clustered vs non-clustered scenarios.
- Outcome logging acceptance:
  - every pitcher-K card emits a loggable record containing: posture, opponent K bucket, leash bucket, projected Ks, game date
  - schema supports future aggregation of: hit rate by posture, over-vs-under bias detection, calibration by leash bucket
  - logged records must be queryable by game date, posture label, and leash bucket
