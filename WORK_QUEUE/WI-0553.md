# WI-0553: Gate FIRST_PERIOD market on edge, not projection signal

**ID**: WI-0553
**Goal**: Remove the projection-signal-only bypass for FIRST_PERIOD markets and require a market price + computed edge before any FIRST_PERIOD card reaches CHEDDAR status.

**Scope**:
- `packages/models/src/decision-pipeline-v2.js`
- `packages/models/src/__tests__/` (add FIRST_PERIOD edge gate tests)
- `web/src/__tests__/api-results-decision-segmentation.test.js` (if FIRST_PERIOD appears in results)

**Out of scope**:
- Changes to FIRST_PERIOD data ingestion or odds fetching
- Changing the `FIRST_PERIOD_POLICY` object name/structure — just its behavior
- Any market type other than FIRST_PERIOD

**Background**:
`FIRST_PERIOD_POLICY.toSharpPriceStatus(projectionSignal)` currently maps `PLAY → CHEDDAR` with no market price required (`marketRequiresPrice` returns false for FIRST_PERIOD). `computeOfficialStatus` delegates entirely to `FIRST_PERIOD_POLICY.toOfficialStatus(projectionSignal)` — no edge comparison, no implied probability check, no price quality gate. This is a VEGAS hard stop: "projection-only play with no market-mispricing thesis." A PLAY can be emitted for FIRST_PERIOD with zero edge computation. The data path for 1P total line and price already exists in the pipeline (around line 637 of decision-pipeline-v2.js).

**Acceptance**:
- `classifyPrice` no longer short-circuits to CHEDDAR/COTTAGE for FIRST_PERIOD based on projectionSignal alone.
- `marketRequiresPrice` returns `true` for FIRST_PERIOD (or FIRST_PERIOD is removed from any price-exemption list).
- FIRST_PERIOD cards are routed through `computeTotalEdge` using the 1P total line and price, with `lean_edge_min` applied the same as the TOTAL market.
- A FIRST_PERIOD card with no market price present is classified as COTTAGE/PASS, not CHEDDAR/PLAY.
- A FIRST_PERIOD card with edge below `lean_edge_min` is classified LEAN at most.
- Existing tests that assume FIRST_PERIOD CHEDDAR without a price are updated to provide a price and edge.
