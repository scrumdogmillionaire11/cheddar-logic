**Goal**: Extend health monitoring to include POTD pipeline status and near-miss shadow performance, surfaced in both Dr. Claire outputs and `/admin`.

**Acceptance**:

- Dr. Claire report includes a POTD health section with explicit status and actionable signals.
- POTD health captures at minimum: last successful run recency, today fired/no-pick state, candidate volume, and near-miss shadow settlement freshness.
- `/api/admin/model-health` includes POTD health payload without breaking existing sport snapshot shape for current consumers.

- `/admin` renders a dedicated POTD health card/panel that includes near-miss status counts and recency indicators.
- Admin/read-path handling is resilient to partial historical POTD data (missing rows should degrade to `no-data`/`stale`, not crash).
