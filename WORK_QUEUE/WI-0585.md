**Acceptance**:
- NHL SOG prop payloads emit a canonical prop decision block with verdict, lean side, market math, trend label, and deterministic reason text.
- NHL SOG odds ingest persists canonical American prices and preserves same-player same-book threshold ladders without mixing line/price pairs.
- NHL SOG prop pricing treats integer and half-point SOG lines as threshold markets (`3+`, `4+`) for fair probability, edge, EV, and downstream verdict semantics.
- Projection-conflicting priced sides are downgraded to `NO PLAY`, flagged with `PROJECTION_CONFLICT`, and explained explicitly instead of surfacing as actionable leans.
- Props API/transform/UI prefer the new prop decision block, keep legacy fallback behavior for old rows, and render `PLAY`, `WATCH`, `NO PLAY`, and `PROJECTION` distinctly.
- The props card hierarchy reads as verdict/status first, projection plus threshold hit-rate second, real edge math third when available, and L5/details after that.
- The Edge box renders only when real fair/implied/edge/EV values exist; it is hidden entirely when market math is unavailable.
- `NO PLAY` rows render unified status-dominant lean copy such as `NO PLAY — Lean Over 3.0`, and stronger non-play leans are visually differentiated from weak ones.
- L5 text interprets the recent average relative to the line (`above line`, `below line`, `near line`) instead of only showing a trend label.
- Strong projection-gap rows that fail on price explain the pricing blocker directly, expose deterministic watchlist triggers when a real better number would qualify, and surface sportsbook source context when available.
- Default props-mode browsing includes PASS-backed `NO PLAY` / `PROJECTION` rows instead of hiding them by default, while explicit actionable-only presets may still narrow the board.
- Prop rows sort by verdict rank, then positive edge quality, then line delta, with stronger non-play projection gaps surfacing ahead of flatter no-play rows.
- Focused worker and web tests cover the new verdict contract, legacy fallback, props UI hierarchy, and non-play differentiation.
