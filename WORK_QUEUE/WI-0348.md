# WI-0348: Market-Specific Edge Detection Hardening (Wave-1 Forward-Only)

**ID**: WI-0348
**Goal**: Enforce exact-wager, market-specific edge pricing for wave-1 PLAY cards and prevent cross-market/proxy edge promotion.

**Acceptance**:
- Wave-1 spread/total/team-total/puckline cards cannot be priced from moneyline win probability fallback.
- Missing market-specific pricing inputs produce unpriced/pass behavior with explicit reason codes and no synthetic `0.0%` edge rendering.
- Priced edge display is rendered only when `p_fair`, `p_implied`, and `edge_pct` are all valid; unpriced cards remain visible as non-priced context.
- Payload traceability includes exact-wager pricing metadata including `line_source` and `price_source`.
- Proxy-tagged cards are not promoted to priced PLAY/LEAN unless explicitly allowed.
- Legacy fallback display and inference paths are removed for wave-1 cards; `decision_v2` is the sole verdict source.
