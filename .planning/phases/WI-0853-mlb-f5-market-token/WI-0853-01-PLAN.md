<objective>
Introduce `FIRST_5_INNINGS` as the canonical market token for MLB F5 (first 5 innings) cards in the worker layer, replacing the semantically incorrect `FIRST_PERIOD` token. All consumer functions (calibration, settlement, audit) must accept both the new `FIRST_5_INNINGS` token and the legacy `FIRST_PERIOD` token to maintain backward compatibility with rows already in the database.

Purpose: `FIRST_PERIOD` implies hockey/basketball "first period" — it's misleading for baseball and creates confusion in the `/api/games` diagnostic output. `FIRST_5_INNINGS` is unambiguous.

Output: run_mlb_model.js emits new token; 6 shared-logic files accept both tokens; NHL paths are untouched.
</objective>
