<objective>
Create `execution-gate.js` with a stateless `evaluateExecution` function that
deducts vig and slippage from raw model edge before allowing a bet signal to
propagate. Wire it into all three sport runners before the card-write step.

Purpose: A model with raw edge +2.5% against -110 juice is a -2% net bet. The
gate enforces net-positive edge, fresh odds, and MODEL_OK status as preconditions
for FIRE/PLAY classification.

Output: New `execution-gate.js`; gate wired in NBA/NHL/MLB runners; `execution_gate`
field on all market-call card payloads; 5 unit tests.
</objective>
