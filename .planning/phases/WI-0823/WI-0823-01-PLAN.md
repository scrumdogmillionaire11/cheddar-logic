<objective>
Consolidate GSaX and raw SV% into a single `resolveGoalieComposite` function that
both the pace model (`applyGoalieAdj`) and the cross-market driver (`goalieSignal`)
consume. Eliminates structural double-counting where goalie quality is applied once
as a scoring multiplier and again as a signal driver.

Purpose: Fixes the WI-0823 audit finding: r≈0.65–0.75 correlation between GSaX and
SV% means using both independently overstates goalie impact in soft-scoring games.

Output: `resolveGoalieComposite` exported from nhl-pace-model.js; both callers updated;
4 unit tests covering all source paths.
</objective>
