# Project Brief: Cheddar Logic LLC

*Generated: January 16, 2026*

## Executive Summary

**Cheddar Logic LLC** is a sports analytics intelligence service that provides data-driven insights to sports enthusiasts without requiring them to perform complex analysis themselves. The company's core differentiator is the "no play is a play" philosophy - actively identifying when restraint is the optimal decision, setting it apart from typical sports services that push constant action.

Initially focused on NBA analytics with a proven working model, Cheddar Logic targets bettors, fantasy players, and data enthusiasts through Discord-based delivery, with plans to expand to professional website tiers and innovative AI-powered responsibility features. The dual business model includes primary sports analytics services (80% focus) and secondary custom web development (20% focus), operating under LLC protection while maintaining strict compliance through responsible messaging and harm reduction positioning.

**Key Value Proposition:** "Where Data Meets Profit Logic" - delivering analytical insights and projection confidence vs market expectations while promoting responsible decision-making.

## Problem Statement

Sports enthusiasts face several critical challenges when trying to make informed decisions:

**Current Pain Points:**
- **Analysis Overload:** Complex statistical analysis requires significant time investment and expertise that most users lack
- **Information Scatter:** Relevant data sources are fragmented across multiple platforms and require manual aggregation
- **Market Inefficiency Recognition:** Identifying where personal analysis differs from market consensus requires sophisticated analytical capabilities
- **Emotional Decision Making:** Lack of objective frameworks leads to impulsive, emotion-driven choices
- **Responsibility Gap:** Most sports services promote constant action without considering user welfare or decision quality

**Impact Quantification:**
- Sports analytics market growing at 31% CAGR, indicating massive demand for better insights
- Fantasy sports industry worth $8.2B+ annually with users spending hours on research
- Betting market inefficiencies create substantial value opportunities for informed participants

**Existing Solution Shortfalls:**
- Traditional tipsters focus on picks rather than decision frameworks
- Free analysis lacks depth and personalization
- Paid services often promote excessive action rather than selective opportunities
- No services emphasize the "no play" decision as a strategic choice

## Proposed Solution
