# Privacy Policy | Cheddar Logic

## **Compliance & Legal**

Cheddar Logic LLC operates an **abstention-first, decision-support analytics platform**.

We collect the **minimum data necessary** to operate the service.
We do **not** sell, rent, or broker user data—ever.

---

## **Privacy Overview**

We process personal data only when necessary for:

* Site functionality and security (**legitimate interest**)
* Subscription services (**contractual necessity**)
* Community participation (**user consent**)

We do not:

* Profile users for advertising
* Track betting behavior
* Sell or share personal data for marketing

---

## **Information We Collect**

### Contact Submissions

* Name
* Email
* Message content

Retention: up to **90 days**, unless required for support follow-up.

---

### Community Access (Optional)

* Discord username
* Role/status within the community

Only collected when you choose to join.

---

### Subscription Data (Future)

* Billing information handled by **Stripe**
* Cheddar Logic does **not store card details**

---

### Analytics (Minimal)

* Aggregated performance metrics
* Infrastructure monitoring data

We do **not**:

* Track individual behavior
* Build user profiles
* Use third-party ad trackers

---

## 🚫 **What We Do NOT Collect**

We explicitly do **not** collect:

* Betting activity or wager history
* Financial account details beyond billing processors
* Behavioral tracking data for advertising
* Sensitive personal data

---

## **How We Use Information**

We apply strict purpose limitation:

* Respond to inquiries and support requests
* Manage community access and enforce conduct standards
* Process subscriptions and payments
* Maintain platform security and prevent abuse

We do **not** use data for:

* Advertising
* Resale
* Behavioral targeting

---

## **Data Sharing**

We only share data with essential service providers required to operate the platform.

We do **not**:

* Sell personal data
* Share data with advertisers
* Provide data to third parties for marketing

---

## **Your Rights**

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may request:

* Access to your data
* Correction or deletion
* Restriction of processing
* Data export

Requests can be submitted via our contact page.
